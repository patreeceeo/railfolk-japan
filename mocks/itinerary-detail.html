Education cards

Shinkansen vs limited express

Shinkansen routes are high-speed intercity services, while limited express trains often connect regional destinations with fewer stops than local services.

Rural bus caution

Rural buses may run infrequently. Check timetables before committing to a same-day transfer.

Onsen basics

Most onsen require washing before entering the bath, and many facilities restrict swimwear.
