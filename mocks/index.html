Railfolk Japan

A lightweight site for creating and sharing Japan train and bus itineraries with curated cultural visits and education cards.

The focus is over-land intercity travel in Japan: no bookings, no live transit data, no route calculation, and no maps.

Browse public itineraries

How it works

  1. Create an itinerary with a title, description, and visibility.
  2. Add dated train, shinkansen, limited express, or bus legs from the curated transit library.
  3. Attach curated visit cards and education cards.
  4. Review the date-grouped itinerary generated from attached item dates.

Sample region

Initial content focuses on Northern Kyushu, including Fukuoka, Nagasaki, Kumamoto, Takeo Onsen, Yufuin, and Beppu.
